About Us

THE CLUB WAS ESTABLISHED TO LET ALL MEMBERS TO GROW TOGETHER!

The New Era University College basketball association closed in 2020 due to the impact of covid and will not officially open again until 2022 - but its core philosophy has been brewing for decades.

founding members Haw Eng Hong, Ng Zhi Tong, Alex Hu Rui Chen and Cheah Yu Heng were all good friends who met on the school basketball court. As time passed, their friendship deepened—and it led them to decide to spend their spare time running the club together.

In order to allow the members of the club, regardless of gender, strong or weak, to have games to play, the founder of the club, Haw Eng Hong, can also be said to spend all his free time to complete this work, but fortunately, the members are in every match. None of the performances let him down, so it makes him feel like he's accomplished!

So in order for all the members to know each other effectively and grow up; the club basically has activity time every night (this is done so that all club members have the opportunity to participate in activities, play games and train together), and there will be Every day, the senior management drives everyone to come to the basketball court to play basketball in the group.

Finally, for the founder of the club, Haw Eng Hong, he knows that everyone hopes to have a chance. Even if they come to university, everyone will develop their own knowledge; but he knows that everyone has a dream, the dream of basketball! Even if it's just a friendly match, he knows that everyone wants a chance to show themselves. So, if you have a dream, then welcome to join our big family (New Era Basketball association)!

Today, the basketball association has always exceeded 100 club members in just one semester, and has become the club with the largest number of people in the school. It has also participated in six games of allsizes.

As NEUC BA continues to expand our footprint, we still keep three guiding principles in mind: grow stronger together, create opportunities for members to express themselves and fulfill their dreams, and support schools that help us as much as possible.

"I WANT TO FORM A BIG FAMILY WHERE EVERYONE CAN PLAY, NO MATTER HOW STRONG OR WEAK THEY ARE!"

- NG ZHI TONG, PRESIDENT
